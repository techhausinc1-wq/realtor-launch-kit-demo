5 things to do BEFORE you get pre-approved

First-time buyers — please don't apply for any new credit (car loan, store card, even an Amex) in the 90 days before you get pre-approved. It tanks your score temporarily. Other 4 things: pull your credit report (free), stash 3 months of bank statements, document any large deposits, keep your job. Questions? Drop them in comments — I'll answer for free.
